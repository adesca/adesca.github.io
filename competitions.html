Mix & Match

Mix & Matches are fun social dance competitions where you join as an individual. In the prelims, you will dance with random partners and be rotated in heats; each dancer is judged individually. In the finals, you will randomly draw a partner and be judged as a couple. The finals will be done in spotlight format.

Solo Jazz

In this competition, contestants will be judged for their solo jazz skills, placing improvisation and artistic awareness high in the judging criteria. We're looking forward to witnessing the breadth of jazz expression! What can you create?

Jedi Swing Ep. IV:
A New Hop

A Lindy Jedi's strength flows from the force through movement and partner connection. This will be a competition in which contestants will enter as a couple and will social dance to a variety of songs, tempos, commands, and challenges.
